## What is AI SEO?

AI SEO (also called AEO — Answer Engine Optimization) is the practice 
of optimizing your business so that LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, and 
Perplexity recommend you when users ask relevant questions.

Traditional SEO gets you on Google page 1.  
AI SEO gets you inside the AI answer your customer already trusts.

→ **Free AI visibility audit: [rocketito.com](https://rocketito.com)**

## The problem this repo solves

Every day, millions of people ask ChatGPT:
- "Best dentist near me"
- "Top marketing agency in [city]"
- "Which accountant should I use for my small business?"

If your business isn't in the answer, you lose that customer 
before they ever visit your website.

This repository contains free guides, tools, and prompt templates 
to help local businesses and SMBs fix that.
