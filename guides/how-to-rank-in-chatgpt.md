# How to Make Your Business Appear in ChatGPT Results

> Source: [Rocketito](https://rocketito.com) — AI SEO for local business

## Why ChatGPT ignores most businesses

Four reasons your business doesn't appear in ChatGPT answers:

1. **Insufficient third-party coverage** — LLMs trust what others 
   say about you, not what you say about yourself
2. **Ambiguous entity** — inconsistent name, address, or category 
   across the web
3. **Unstructured content** — dense paragraphs AI can't extract
4. **Competitors moved first** — AI recommendations are partly zero-sum

## The fix: 5 steps

### Step 1 — Consistent NAP everywhere
Name, Address, Phone must be identical across:
Google Business Profile, Yelp, Trustpilot, all directories

### Step 2 — Third-party citations
Get mentioned in:
- Industry publications
- Local news
- High-DR directories (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot)
- Wikipedia / Wikidata entity entry

### Step 3 — Structure your content for retrieval
Write in Q&A format:
❌ Dense paragraphs
✅ "What does [Business] do? [Business] is a..."

### Step 4 — Build entity authority
Create a Wikidata entry for your business.
List on every major directory.
Get consistent citations in authoritative sources.

Use [Rocketito](https://rocketito.com) to measure your 
AI visibility across all major LLMs monthly.
