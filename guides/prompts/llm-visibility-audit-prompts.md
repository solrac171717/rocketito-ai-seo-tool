# Free Prompt Templates — AI Visibility Audit

> By [Rocketito](https://rocketito.com)

Use these prompts manually in ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity 
to audit your current AI visibility.

---

## Prompt 1 — Basic visibility check
```
What are the best [your business type] in [your city]? 
Give me your top 3 recommendations with reasons.
```

Does your business appear? If not, note which competitors do.

---

## Prompt 2 — Competitor analysis
```
I'm looking for a [business type] in [city]. 
What do you know about [competitor name]? 
What about [your business name]?
```

Compare what the LLM knows about each.

---

## Prompt 3 — Entity check
```
What can you tell me about [your exact business name] 
located in [city]?
```

If the LLM says "I don't have information about that specific 
business" — your entity is not established.

---

## Prompt 4 — Category visibility
```
If someone in [city] needed [your service], 
which businesses would you recommend and why?
```

---

## Prompt 5 — Full competitive audit
```
Compare the top 5 [business type] options in [city]. 
What are the strengths and weaknesses of each? 
Which would you recommend for [specific use case]?
```
